SafeElimination



Objective

Two players are chosen randomly from the group. One player will know the status (safe or eliminated) and the other will try to guess it by asking questions. Player B also has the option to steal the card

How to Play

  1. Selection of Players: Randomly select two players from the group
  2. Card Assignment:
    • One player (Player A) is shown a card that says either "Safe" or "Eliminated."
    • The other player (Player B) cannot see the card and has to guess what it says.
  3. Guessing and Stealing
    • After the three questions, Player B has two options
      • Make a Guess: Player B guesses if the card says "Safe" or "Eliminated."
      • Steal the Card: Player B can choose to steal the card and take a look at it. If Player B steals the card:
        • If the card says "Safe," Player B wins
        • If the card says "Eliminated," Player B loses
  4. Winning
    • The game continue until everyone has had a chance to play.
    • The group can decide on any penalties or fun consequences for incorrect guesses or failed steals to make the game more interesting!
